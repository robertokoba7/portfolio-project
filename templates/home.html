About Mwanga Float

Mwanga Float is revolutionizing mobile money transfer services by implementing a serialized banknotes traceability system that sets a new standard for fraud prevention. With this system, we're able to provide our users with the highest level of security and peace of mind, knowing that every transaction is tracked and monitored from start to finish. By using unique serialization codes, we're able to quickly identify and investigate any suspicious activity, and take immediate action to prevent fraud and protect our users. At Mwanga Float, we're committed to delivering a safe and reliable platform that makes mobile money transfer easy and accessible for everyone
